Comprehensive Travel Guide: Colombo (CMB) to Italy

Overview

  • This guide provides a reliable and cost-efficient route from Colombo (CMB) to Italy via Dubai (DXB) or Abu Dhabi (AUH) and Zagreb (ZAG).
  • It leverages Croatia’s non-Schengen status to simplify immigration and provides seamless travel to Italy by land or sea.

Step-by-Step Route Details

Step 1: Colombo (CMB) to Dubai (DXB) or Abu Dhabi (AUH)

  • Airlines:
    • Emirates (Direct: CMB → DXB)
    • Etihad Airways (Direct: CMB → AUH)
  • Flight Duration: ~4.5 hours
  • Cost: $400 - $600
  • Immigration: None during layovers in Dubai or Abu Dhabi.

Step 2: Dubai (DXB) or Abu Dhabi (AUH) to Zagreb (ZAG)

  • Airlines:
    • Flydubai (Direct DXB → ZAG)
    • Qatar Airways + Croatia Airlines
  • Flight Duration: ~6.5 hours
  • Cost: $300 - $500
  • Immigration:
    • Entry occurs at Zagreb Airport (Croatia, EU but non-Schengen).
    • Passport control for Spain passport holders is simplified.

Step 3: Zagreb (ZAG) to Italy

Option A: By Ferry

  • Domestic Travel to Croatian Ports:
    • Travel by train, bus, or domestic flight to Dubrovnik or Rijeka (~8-9 hours).
    • Cost: $30 - $50
  • Ferry Routes:
    • Dubrovnik → Bari (Italy): ~10 hours, $50 - $100
    • Rijeka → Venice (Italy): ~6 hours, $50 - $100

Option B: By Land

  • Travel Mode: Train or bus to Trieste or Venice (~4-7 hours).
  • Cost: $20 - $50
  • Immigration: None due to open EU borders.
